Center - to be placed in between bed profiles. It is supposed to catch debris from the brush  
  
Front access - Bucket shaped in such way, that it can be slid under the bed all the way to the front and pulled out of printer. 
Can be removed without lifting the gantry, but does not have locking mechanism to frame. You can either leave it loose, or you can secure it with self adhesive velcro.

Rear access - Bucked designed to be removed by moving it towards back and lifting it along rear panel. Has locking to frame by clips, but requires to lift gantry high up before emptying.

Center bucket and Front access buckets were designed by AmfKuna
